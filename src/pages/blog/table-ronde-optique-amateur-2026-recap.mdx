The first edition of the *Table ronde optique amateur francophone* took place on March 4-5, 2026, on the Astro-FR Discord server.

Big thanks to speakers who accepted to prepare and present, to the Astro-FR crew for hosting us, and to everyone who showed up and asked questions.

Slides and videos are being published as they come in. The full archive is here:

[**→ table-ronde-optique-amateur.fr/archive/2026**](https://table-ronde-optique-amateur.fr/archive/2026)

## Schedule

### March 4

- **Jean-Pierre Gelabert** — *Une table à polir les miroirs de télescopes.* Building a machine to take the drudgery out of rough grinding, fine grinding and polishing, in a few hours of DIY work. [[video]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4uqjfINiJio)
- **Lucas Sifoni** — *DFTFringe live : un orchestrateur Elixir pour l'interférométrie.* An alternative "live" build of DFTFringe, making interferometry a bit more near-real-time. [[slides]](https://table-ronde-optique-amateur.fr/archive/2026/2026-lucas-sifoni.pdf) [[video]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ii2eGb7vbk4)
- **Victor Poughon** — *Torch Lens Maker: conception d'optiques en Python.* An open-source Python library for modeling and designing optical systems, built on PyTorch and differentiable geometrical optics. [[slides]](https://table-ronde-optique-amateur.fr/archive/2026/2026-victor-poughon.pdf) [[video]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VmRXLaRgI4c)
- **Alain Spang** — *Premiers essais avec la fonction de transfert optique différentielle.* First results with a differential OTF method: two images of the same star, one with a slightly modified aperture, to evaluate mild instrument defects. [[slides]](https://table-ronde-optique-amateur.fr/archive/2026/2026-alain-spang.pdf) [[video]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TJjsghsUdJs)

### March 5

- **Paul Faÿs-Long** — *Comment tester des petites surfaces très ouvertes.* Traditional methods of testing lose precision on very fast and/or very small surfaces, but a micro-tester can help us. [[slides]](https://table-ronde-optique-amateur.fr/archive/2026/2026-paul-fays-long.pdf) [[video]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QjQrM00cH7U)
- **Denis Bernard** — *Quel avenir pour les sténopés ?* Against the usual framing of the pinhole as a primitive, "alternative" film device. [[slides]](https://table-ronde-optique-amateur.fr/archive/2026/2026-denis-bernard.pdf) [[video]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=X1XWRQohdEA)
- **Eric Royer** — *Polissage de miroirs plans.* Polishing your own secondary mirrors, drawing from experience making the two flats for his 600mm binoscope. [[slides]](https://table-ronde-optique-amateur.fr/archive/2026/2026-eric-royer.pdf)
- **Raphaël Labro** — *Construire sa lunette astronomique avec du verre bas de gamme.* Building an artisanal refractor around a commodity 80/600 achromatic doublet, and squeezing as much as possible out of it. [[slides]](https://table-ronde-optique-amateur.fr/archive/2026/2026-raphael-labro.pdf)
